What is the GovLab Academy

The goal of the GovLab Academy is to support a new generation of public entrepreneurs to tap the intelligence of citizens and create actionable projects that improve people’s lives.

  • Build an open-access library of interactive resources focused on innovations in smarter governance
  • Grow a network of like-minded collaborators working on public problems and widen the pool of mentors
  • Via the ‘Accelerator’ program the Academy offers structured programs which provide would-be civic innovators with the training, practical assistance and support they need to get their project ready to launch. This programming is delivered face-to-face and online.
